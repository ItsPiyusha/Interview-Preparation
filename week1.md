# Daily Workflow Example

**Morning:**
- Choose DSA topic (e.g., Trees).
- List 3-5 problems to solve.

**During Study:**
- Briefly explain your plan in a short video/voice note.
- Record screen/code as you work through tough problems (could be sped up and commented later).
- Make notes of mistakes or new techniques — save screenshots.

**Post Study:**
- Write a summary (text, audio, video):
  - Today’s topic.
  - Problems solved (LeetCode #, difficulty).
  - Main lessons.
  - Any frustration or questions for future review.

**Weekly:**
- Record a reflection clip comparing progress against goals.
- Pick one problem to explain step-by-step, as if teaching.

# Tips:
- Don’t worry about being perfect—show your real struggles and wins!
- Keep “raw” documentation for yourself and edit for YouTube later.
- Always keep a backup of your notes, screenshots, and clips.

# Outcome
- At the end, you have not only a polished video series, but also useful notes and lessons for interviews!
